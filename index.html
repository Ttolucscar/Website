    When Did You Start?

    When I was seven years old.

    I began creating art in kindergarten at seven, so it has been ten years. However, I am not currently making any art except for school projects.

    How Did You Learn?

    Watching cartoons.

    Seeing others' artwork online really inspired me to learn how to draw. I always tried copying the art style of the cartoons I watched when I was young.

    Art has always been an enormous part of my life, especially when I was younger. It has helped me express myself, not in words but through imagery, especially at difficult times. It has taught me to appreciate my surroundings despite disaster or chaos. Each person interprets art in their own unique way, which contributes to its beauty. I may not be able to create as much art as I once did, but I would love to start drawing again and relearning how to draw using old and new techniques.
